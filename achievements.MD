# Rings of the Damned – Achievements & Weapon Unlocks

This table shows all achievements players must complete to unlock weapons in *Rings of the Damned*. Each achievement is a **two-step unlock**: complete the achievement → weapon becomes available.

# Rings of the Damned – Achievements, Weapon Unlocks & Locations

This table shows achievements players must complete to unlock weapons in *Rings of the Damned*, with the location or Circle where each weapon can be obtained.

| Achievement | Unlock Condition / Description | Weapon Reward | Circle / Location |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- | ---------------- |
| Protector of Souls | Summon all Ribs of Adam allies in a single combat encounter without them dying. | Ribs of Adam | Divine Realm / Angelic Tower |
| Eden's Trial | Survive a burning tiles challenge for 60 seconds without dying. | Flaming Sword of Eden | Garden of Eden (Early-game) |
| Angel's Trial | Complete a timed mobility course using only jumps, dashes, and flight boosts. | Michael’s Wings | Angelic Realm (Mobility Course) |
| Choir of the Damned | Defeat the Choir miniboss in Angelic Tower without taking AoE damage. | Trumpet of Judgment | Angelic Tower |
| Martyr's Path | Equip Nails of Crucifixion and defeat 50 enemies without using healing items. | Nails of Crucifixion | Symbolic Relic Chamber |
| Pain is Power | Equip Thorn Whip and defeat a boss while taking at least 20% max HP damage. | Thorn Whip | Hell-mode Optional Boss |
| Cain’s Descent | Defeat Cain in Hell-mode without dying. | Cain’s Shard | Circle 1 – Limbo |
| David’s Test | Complete a precision challenge hitting all moving targets with Sling of David. | Sling of David | Circle 2 – Lust |
| Pharaoh of Pestilence | Defeat the plague-casting boss without leaving the arena. | Moses’ Staff | Circle 3 – Gluttony |
| Samson’s Trial | Defeat Samson using only Jawbone of Samson. | Jawbone of Samson | Circle 4 – Greed |
| Circle of Suffering | Survive a Hell-mode encounter using only Crown of Thorns until all enemies are defeated. | Crown of Thorns | Circle 5 – Wrath |
| Divine Archive | Find and interact with all Ten Commandments tablets in a single playthrough. | Ten Commandments | Circle 6 – Heresy |
| Forbidden Library | Collect all pages of the cursed tome Ink of the Scribe. | Ink of the Scribe | Circle 7 – Violence |
| Damned | Defeat each Circle boss in Damned mode (Limbo → Treachery). | Limbo: Forgotten Blade, Lust: Whip of Desire, Gluttony: Maw Gauntlets, Greed: Gilded Chainaxe, Wrath: Bloody Brass Knuckles, Heresy: Blasphemer’s Brand, Violence: Bloodletter Pike, Fraud: Mirror Blade, Treachery: Dagger of Judas | Circles 1–9 |
| Eden’s Guardian | Beat the final boss on Very Hard difficulty without dying. | SBR (Single Bullet Rifle) | Final Boss / Nightmare Difficulty |
