# Rings of the Damned – Achievements & Weapon Unlocks

This table shows all achievements players must complete to unlock weapons in *Rings of the Damned*. Each achievement is a **two-step unlock**: complete the achievement → weapon becomes available.

| Achievement | Unlock Condition / Description | Weapon Reward |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- |
| Protector of Souls | Summon all Ribs of Adam allies in a single combat encounter without them dying. | Ribs of Adam |
| Eden's Trial | Survive a burning tiles challenge for 60 seconds without dying. | Flaming Sword of Eden |
| Angel's Trial | Complete a timed mobility course using only jumps, dashes, and flight boosts. | Michael’s Wings |
| Choir of the Damned | Defeat the Choir miniboss in Angelic Tower without taking AoE damage. | Trumpet of Judgment |
| Martyr's Path | Equip Nails of Crucifixion and defeat 50 enemies without using healing items. | Nails of Crucifixion |
| Pain is Power | Equip Thorn Whip and defeat a boss while taking at least 20% max HP damage. | Thorn Whip |
| Cain’s Descent | Defeat Cain in Hell-mode without dying. | Cain’s Shard |
| David’s Test | Complete a precision challenge hitting all moving targets with Sling of David. | Sling of David |
| Pharaoh of Pestilence | Defeat the plague-casting boss without leaving the arena. | Moses’ Staff |
| Samson’s Trial | Defeat Samson using only Jawbone of Samson. | Jawbone of Samson |
| Circle of Suffering | Survive a Hell-mode encounter using only Crown of Thorns until all enemies are defeated. | Crown of Thorns |
| Divine Archive | Find and interact with all Ten Commandments tablets in a single playthrough. | Ten Commandments |
| Forbidden Library | Collect all pages of the cursed tome Ink of the Scribe. | Ink of the Scribe |
| Hell-mode Circle Bosses | Defeat each Circle boss in Hell-mode (Limbo → Treachery). | Limbo: Forgotten Blade, Lust: Whip of Desire, Gluttony: Maw Gauntlets, Greed: Gilded Chainaxe, Wrath: Bloody Brass Knuckles, Heresy: Blasphemer’s Brand, Violence: Bloodletter Pike, Fraud: Mirror Blade, Treachery: Dagger of Judas |
| Eden’s Guardian | Beat the final boss on Very Hard difficulty without dying. | SBR (Single Bullet Rifle) |
