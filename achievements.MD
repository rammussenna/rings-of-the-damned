# Rings of the Damned – Achievements & Weapon Unlocks

This table shows all achievements players must complete to unlock weapons in *Rings of the Damned*. Each achievement is a **two-step unlock**: complete the achievement → weapon becomes available.

| Achievement | Unlock Condition / Description | Weapon Reward |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- |
| Protector of Souls | Dont kill any npc's and dont damage judas when transformed into a past allie | Ribs of Adam |
| Eden's Trial | Survive a burning tiles challenge for 60 seconds without dying. | Flaming Sword of Eden |
| Angel's Trial | Complete a timed mobility course using only jumps, dashes, and flight boosts. | Michael’s Wings |
| Choir of the Damned | Defeat the Choir miniboss in limbo without taking any damage. | Trumpet of Judgment |
| Martyr's Path | Equip Cruciform Pike and defeat 100 enemies without using healing items and losing any health. | Nails of Crucifixion |
| Cain’s Descent | Defeat Cain in Damned mode without dying. | Cain’s Shard |
| David’s Test | Complete a precision challenge hitting all moving targets with Sling of David. | Sling of David |
| Pharaoh of Pestilence | Defeat the plague casting mini boss without leaving the arena. | Moses’ Staff |
| Samson’s Trial | Defeat Samson using only Jawbone of Samson. | Jawbone of Samson |
| Circle of Suffering | Damned mode reward after beating the mini bosses in wrath. | Crown of Thorns |
| Divine Archive | Find and interact with all Ten Commandments tablets in a single playthrough. | Ten Commandments |
| Forbidden Library | Collect all pages of the cursed tome Ink of the Scribe. | Ink of the Scribe |
| Damned mode Circle Bosses | Defeat each Circle boss in Damned mode (Limbo → Treachery). | Limbo: Forgotten Blade, Lust: Whip of Desire, Gluttony: Maw Gauntlets, Greed: Gilded Chainaxe, Wrath: Bloody Brass Knuckles, Heresy: Blasphemer’s Brand, Violence: Bloodletter Pike, Fraud: Mirror Blade, Treachery: Dagger of Judas |
| Eden’s Guardian | Beat the final boss on Abyssal difficulty without dying. | SBR (Single Bullet Rifle) |
