## 1.  **Limbo** ‚Äì *The Hollow King*

* **Appearance**: A regal, ancient figure clad in broken stone armor and veiled in ethereal fog. His face is featureless, replaced by shifting masks of famous philosophers and poets.
* **Lore**: Once a noble ruler of a peaceful age, the Hollow King chose eternal indecision over conflict. Limbo became his domain as a result of passivity.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Philosopher's Reflection**: Reflects ranged attacks if not timed correctly.
  * **Fog Bind**: Temporarily disables dodging.
  * **Time Slow**: Occasionally slows time for the player only.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Uses illusions to disguise real attacks.
  * The arena shifts with statues that "judge" you.
  * Becomes more aggressive as your sins stack.

## 2.  **Lust** ‚Äì *Valentina, the Siren of Echoes*

* **Appearance**: Tall, alluring humanoid with semi-liquid skin that ripples with every movement. Her face constantly morphs into those she once seduced.
* **Lore**: A spirit cursed to constantly seduce and consume. Her voice causes hallucinations of lovers lost.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Siren's Echo**: Fires charm projectiles that cause player to walk toward her.
  * **Mirage Split**: Splits into three mirages. All must be killed quickly or she regenerates health.
  * **Temptation Bloom**: Area-of-effect pulse that lowers player defenses temporarily.
  * **Lustbound Spawn**: At 50% HP, summons other Lust-based enemies. She can kill them herself to absorb their energy.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Charm-based mechanics that alter movement.
  * Prioritizes illusion, distraction, and regeneration.

## 3.  **Gluttony** ‚Äì *Gorvash, the Maw Eternal*

* **Appearance**: Massive, obese demon with multiple mouths across his body and stomachs that open into voids.
* **Lore**: Gorvash devoured every soul he met, even demons. His hunger created a gravitational pull of rot.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Devour Pulse**: Draws the player inward, dealing damage if close.
  * **Stomach Burst**: Shoots acidic bile and chewed corpses.
  * **Cannibal Regeneration**: Eats summoned mobs to regain health.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Player must destroy stomach cores that open intermittently.
  * Arena fills with bile, slowing movement.

## 4. **Greed** ‚Äì *Archon Mammon, Lord of Excess*

* **Appearance**: Gilded demon adorned with chains, gold-plated flesh, and hollow eyes that drip molten gold.
* **Lore**: Mammon was once an angel of structure and trade but fell when he began hoarding worship.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Golden Hoard**: Summons animated gold to block paths or attack.
  * **Auction of Souls**: Debuff based on current item count; more items = more punishment.
  * **Bribe Reality**: Temporarily becomes invulnerable by "paying off" the world.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Richer players are punished more.
  * Loot is bait‚Äîgrabbing it powers him up unless cleansed first.
* **Quote**: ‚ÄúWhat you own... owns you.‚Äù

## 5. ‚ö° **Wrath** ‚Äì *Agramor, the Flame-Tethered*

* **Appearance**: A burning knight wrapped in iron chains and molten plates, missing an arm replaced with a flail.
* **Lore**: Agramor was a fallen general whose fury consumed entire cities. He now burns forever in his rage.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Chain Smash**: Mid-range flail slam that burns.
  * **Rage Flood**: Gains attack speed and movement under 30% HP.
  * **Molten Prison**: Locks player in a burning arena.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Becomes far more dangerous under 30% HP (50% bonus damage).
  * Fight encourages aggressive parrying.

## 6.  **Heresy** ‚Äì *The Red Priestess*

* **Appearance**: A burning figure wrapped in scrolls and heretical texts, blindfolded, with a flaming quill for a hand.
* **Lore**: Once a prophet, she began rewriting divine scripture in her own vision. Now, she writes only in blood.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Scripture Rewrite**: Inverts player controls temporarily.
  * **Hellfire Veil**: Covers arena in blasphemous fire.
  * **Mark of Blasphemy**: Reduces player HP by 50%.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Fight is visual chaos, reading her attacks is harder.
  * Punishes memorization, rewards adaptation.

## 7.  **Violence** ‚Äì *Crucior, the Beast of Red Earth*

* **Appearance**: A hulking demon with weapons embedded in its flesh, constantly dripping blood, screaming.
* **Lore**: Crucior is the manifestation of suffering given form. A former executioner who relished the pain he caused.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Blood Cascade**: Area-wide bleed effect.
  * **Weapon Harvest**: Rips weapons from its own body and throws.
  * **Rage Chains**: Slams chained corpses at the player.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Constant HP loss to area bleed.
  * Requires aggressive movement and use of cover.


---

## 8.  **Fraud** ‚Äì *Miragio the Deceiver*

* **Appearance**: Appears as the player at first. Slowly morphs into grotesque mimic.
* **Lore**: A master con artist who tricked angels and demons alike.
* **Abilities**:

  * **Mirror Dash**: Copies player movement style and attacks.
  * **Clone Storm**: Creates illusions of the player.
  * **False Parry**: Fakes openings, punishes greedy attacks.
* **Fight Mechanics**:

  * Dynamically learns player attack patterns.
  * Mirrors gear and ring effects in real-time.
