
### Core Systems

#### Ring System
- **9 Main Rings**: One per circle, obtained by defeating bosses
- **Ring Fusion**: Combine two rings to create hybrid effects at greater cost
- **Equipped Rings**: Only 1 ring can be equipped at a time
- **Ring Switching**: Can swap rings at safe zones (limited mid-combat availability)
- **Build Identity**: Rings dramatically alter playstyle, NPC reactions, and available routes

#### Combat System
- **Real-time hack & slash** with weapon combos
- **Stamina-based mechanics** (dodging, parrying, heavy attacks)
- **Ring-powered abilities** triggered by equipped ring
- **Parry/Block system** that rewards player skill
- **Environmental hazards** unique to each circle

#### Progression System
- **Linear with optional challenges**: All 9 circles must be completed in order
- **Difficulty scaling**: Enemy HP and damage scale with player progression
- **Mini-bosses**: Optional encounters that grant achievements and lore insights
- **Loot & Economy**: Weapons, healing items, and currency drop from enemies

#### Betrayal Counter (Hidden Mechanic)
Tracks player moral choices:
- Sparing enemies instead of killing them → +5 betrayal
- Using cursed/deceptive rings (Fraud, Treachery) → +3 betrayal
- Trusting NPCs who betray you → +10 betrayal
- Finding/using certain powerful items → +2 betrayal

**Impact on Lucifer**:
- **Low Counter (0-30)**: Lucifer weakened; some NPCs assist during final phase
- **Medium Counter (31-70)**: Baseline difficulty; balanced encounter
- **High Counter (71-100)**: Lucifer empowered; additional attacks, phases, taunts

## 5. THE NINE CIRCLES & BOSS OVERVIEW

### Circle 1: LIMBO - *The Hollow King*
**Sin**: Apathy / Absence  
**Ring Effect**: Immune to fear/stun/confusion | XP gain reduced by 30%  
**Boss Mechanic**: Punishes passive play; illusions reward decisive action  
**Estimated Runtime**: 15-20 minutes

### Circle 2: LUST - *Valentina, the Siren*
**Sin**: Obsession  
**Ring Effect**: +10% attack speed & dash distance | Enemies detect you farther away  
**Boss Mechanic**: Charm-based crowd control; camera lock; regeneration via illusions  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 3: GLUTTONY - *Gorvash, the Maw Eternal*
**Sin**: Excess  
**Ring Effect**: Kills restore HP & stamina | +15% damage taken  
**Boss Mechanic**: Destructible stomach cores; arena fills with bile; healing items empower boss  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 4: GREED - *Archon Mammon, Lord of Excess*
**Sin**: Hoarding  
**Ring Effect**: +30% loot from all sources | Merchant prices increase 20%  
**Boss Mechanic**: Punishes rich players; loot is a trap; invulnerability via "bribery"  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 5: WRATH - *Agramor, the Flame-Tethered*
**Sin**: Rage  
**Ring Effect**: Under 20% HP: +50% damage | Above 20% HP: -40% defense  
**Boss Mechanic**: Heat gauge system; becomes dangerous when low on health; parrying reduces arena heat  
**Estimated Runtime**: 15-20 minutes

### Circle 6: HERESY - *Rhea, The Red Priestess*
**Sin**: Rebellion  
**Ring Effect**: 30% faster mana regen & cooldown reduction | Max HP permanently reduced by 60%  
**Boss Mechanic**: Scripture Rewrite (inverts controls); Hellfire Veil (arena denial); Mark of Blasphemy (conditional punishment)  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 7: VIOLENCE - *Crucior, the Beast of Red Earth*
**Sin**: Brutality  
**Ring Effect**: +25% melee damage & crit chance | Enemies drop less healing/mana items  
**Boss Mechanic**: Past victims scale with player deaths; constant bleed damage; weapon harvesting  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 8: FRAUD - *Miragio the Deceiver*
**Sin**: Deception  
**Ring Effect**: Illusions spawn every 20s (30% damage copies) | Controls randomly invert after taking damage  
**Boss Mechanic**: Mirrors player attacks; dynamically learns patterns; morphs into NPC forms  
**Estimated Runtime**: 20-25 minutes

### Circle 9: TREACHERY - *Lucifer, the First Traitor*
**Sin**: Betrayal  
**Ring Effect**: +100% backstab/surprise attack damage | Front-facing attacks deal 80% less damage  
**Boss Mechanic**: Three-phase encounter; Memory Echoes; Wraith Guardians; Arena Collapse; Betrayal Counter affects difficulty  
**Estimated Runtime**: 30-40 minutes

## 6. RING SYSTEM - DETAILED MECHANICS

### Ring Fusion
**Unlock Point**: After defeating Lust boss  
**Mechanic**: Combine two rings from different circles to create hybrid effects  
**Penalty**: Fused rings apply both curses at amplified values  

**Example Fusion**:
- **Limbo** (Armor/Health +; XP Gain -30%) + **Wrath** (Low HP Damage +50%; Normal HP Defense -40%) = **Ring of Impatience**
  - **Blessing**: High armor, health regeneration on close-range kills
  - **Curse**: Reduced health, armor, and mana

**Fusion Limits**:
- Cannot combine rings from the same circle
- Cannot combine more than 2 rings
- Fused rings cannot be further combined
- Must choose fusion carefully—cannot undo until next playthrough

### Build Archetypes

| Archetype | Recommended Rings | Playstyle |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Tank** | Gluttony, Violence | Tanky brawler; trade damage for survival |
| **Glass Cannon Mage** | Heresy, Lust | Use resources aggressively; burst damage |
| **Stealth/Crit** | Treachery, Fraud | High-risk, high-reward burst attacks |
| **Balanced** | Limbo, Greed | Steady damage; economic focus |
| **Aggressive** | Wrath, Violence | Low HP playstyle; constant damage |

## 7. COMBAT & MECHANICS

### Combat Philosophy
- **Skill-based, not stat-based**: Good parrying/dodging mechanics matter more than gear
- **Punishing but fair**: Attacks are telegraphed; skilled players can read patterns
- **Ring-altering playstyle**: Not just stat changes—rings fundamentally shift how you fight

### Core Mechanics

#### Stamina System
- Actions consume stamina: dodge (15), parry (10), light attack (5), heavy attack (20)
- Stamina regenerates over time (faster with certain rings)
- Out-of-stamina = vulnerable to staggers

#### Parry & Riposte
- Successful parry opens a brief window for high-damage riposte
- Different weapons have different parry windows (swords better than hammers)
- Parrying reduces heat in Wrath boss fight; other situational uses

#### Ring Abilities
Each ring grants a unique active ability (on cooldown):

| Ring | Ability | Effect | Cooldown |
|------|---------|--------|----------|
| Limbo | Certainty | Grants clarity; reveals illusions; +20% damage for 5s | 15s |
| Lust | Charm | Stun nearby enemies for 3s | 20s |
| Gluttony | Gorge | Heal 30% HP; draw nearby items | 25s |
| Greed | Gold Rush | +50% loot for 10s | 30s |
| Wrath | Fury | +100% damage for 5s (costs 50% current HP) | 20s |
| Heresy | Rewrite | Invert controls of nearby enemies for 4s | 25s |
| Violence | Bloodlust | +25% damage & lifesteal for 8s | 30s |
| Fraud | Mimic | Copy enemy attack; use it once | 15s |
| Treachery | Backstab Ready | Next backstab is guaranteed crit + 200% damage | 20s |

### Difficulty Modes

| Difficulty | Player Damage Multiplier | Enemy Damage Multiplier | Enemy Aggression | Ring Curve |
|-----------|-------------------------|------------------------|------------------|------------|
| **Easy** | 1.3x | 0.7x | 70% | Forgiving; all rings viable |
| **Normal** | 1.0x | 1.0x | 100% | Baseline; expect challenge |
| **Hard** | 0.8x | 1.3x | 130% | Expertise required; ring balance critical |
| **Hell** | 0.6x | 1.6x | 160% | Hardcore only; mechanical mastery demanded |

## 8. LORE & WORLD DESIGN

### Setting: The Nine Circles of Hell
A corrupted mirror of Dante's Inferno. Each circle is a self-contained ecosystem embodying its sin:

- **Limbo**: Gray, foggy ruins of an ancient kingdom. Masks and philosophers haunt the landscape.
- **Lust**: Viscous, dreamlike red landscape. Semi-liquid surfaces; constant hallucinations.
- **Gluttony**: A massive feeding ground. Rotting vegetation, open mouths, acidic bile pools.
- **Greed**: Gilded architecture with chains. Mountains of gold and empty treasuries.
- **Wrath**: Volcanic hell. Burning chains, molten lakes, scorched earth.
- **Heresy**: A library on fire. Endless scrolls, divine scripture turned to ash.
- **Violence**: A slaughterhouse. Weapons embedded in earth, blood fountains, wailing voices.
- **Fraud**: A hall of mirrors. Nothing is real; reflections attack; illusions feel solid.
- **Treachery**: Frozen hell. An ice lake with Lucifer entombed. Divine yet corrupted.

### NPCs & Interactions
- **Guides**: Mentors who assist (voice changes based on Fraud boss mechanic)
- **Merchants**: Sell weapons, healing items, ring fragments
- **Questgivers**: Offer side missions that increase/decrease Betrayal Counter
- **Lore Keepers**: Provide world context and sin philosophy

**NPC Consequence System**:
Trusting certain NPCs or sparing enemies increases Betrayal Counter. Some NPCs return in later circles or assist during Lucifer fight if counter is low.

## 14. KNOWN DESIGN CHALLENGES

### Challenge 1: Ring Balance
**Problem**: Some rings are inherently stronger than others (e.g., Treachery +100% backstab is high-risk high-reward, while Limbo is purely defensive).

**Solution**: Ensure every ring has a viable build path. Monitor balance through playtesting. Consider difficulty modifiers per ring.

### Challenge 2: Fusion Explosion
**Problem**: With 9 rings, there are C(9,2) = 36 possible fusions. Balancing all of them is complex.

**Solution**: Set hard limits on fusion combinations. Not all fusions are allowed (e.g., can't fuse two curse-heavy rings). Create fusion tiers.

### Challenge 3: Betrayal Counter Prediction
**Problem**: Players may accidentally (or intentionally) optimize Betrayal Counter, making Lucifer easier than intended.

**Solution**: Keep counter hidden. Make consequences feel organic, not gamified. Ensure even a "perfect" playthrough doesn't trivialize Lucifer.

### Challenge 4: Pacing & Boss Difficulty Curve
**Problem**: 9 bosses in sequence risks fatigue or difficulty spikes.

**Solution**: Vary boss types (tanky, fast, puzzle-like, aerial). Ensure each boss teaches new mechanics. Difficulty should escalate smoothly.

## 15. SUCCESS METRICS & POLISH TARGETS

### Core Design Goals
- ✅ Every ring should be **viable for at least one playstyle**
- ✅ Betrayal Counter should feel **organic, not artificial**
- ✅ Boss fights should feel **skill-based and telegraphed**
- ✅ Fusion should add **meaningful diversity, not overwhelming complexity**
- ✅ Endings should vary **based on Betrayal Counter (3+ variants)**

### Quality Targets
- **Boss clarity**: Player should always know what attack is coming
- **Combat feel**: Hits should feel weighty; dodges should feel responsive
- **Narrative coherence**: Each circle's mechanics should reinforce its thematic sin
- **No softlocks**: Progression should never become impossible due to ring choice
